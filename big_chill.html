BIG CHILL
Species: Necrofriggian

Big Chill is a moth-like alien from the icy planet Kylmyys. He can phase through solid matter, become invisible, and generate freezing temperatures by exhaling a chilling breath capable of freezing enemies and objects solid.

Homeworld
Kylmyys
Abilities
Intangibility, invisibility, cryokinesis, flight
Weakness
Extreme heat can weaken his abilities

First appearance
