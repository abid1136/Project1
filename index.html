OMNITRIX POWER

The Omnitrix contains the DNA of over a million alien species from across the galaxy. With the power to transform into any alien hero, Ben Tennyson protects Earth and the universe from threats beyond imagination.

ALIEN FORCE

Five years later, Ben discovers new aliens and faces greater challenges. With Gwen's magical abilities and Kevin's powers, the team battles the Highbreed invasion and uncovers the secrets of the Omnitrix's true potential.

ALIEN X

Alien X is a Celestialsapien, a nigh-omnipotent, reality-warping alien species from the Ben 10 franchise. As one of Ben Tennyson's most powerful transformations, Alien X is capable of altering reality, time, and space with just a thought, but this immense power comes with a significant and difficult-to-manage weakness.

BIG CHILL

Big Chill is one of Ben Tennyson's transformations in the Ben 10 franchise, first appearing in Ben 10: Alien Force. As a Necrofriggian from the planet Kylmyys, he possesses a combination of cryokinesis (ice manipulation) and intangibility abilities.

DIAMOND HEAD

Diamondhead is the Omnitrix's DNA sample of a Petrosapien, a silicon-based alien species from the destroyed planet Petropia. As one of Ben Tennyson's original ten aliens, he has been a consistently reliable and powerful transformation throughout the Ben 10 franchise

ECHO ECHO

Echo Echo is Ben Tennyson's transformation of a Sonorosian, a species of silicon-based lifeforms from the planet Sonorosia. Unlike Ditto, whose clones are connected and share pain, Echo Echo's duplicates are independent and can be destroyed without affecting the others.

HEATBLAST

Heatblast is the very first alien Ben Tennyson transforms into in the Ben 10 franchise. He is a Pyronite from the planet-like star Pyros and has a body made of rock and magma. \

UPGRADE

Upgrade is the Omnitrix's DNA sample of a Galvanic Mechamorph, a technological, liquid-metal alien species from the moon Galvan B. As one of Ben Tennyson's original ten aliens, he possesses the ability to merge with, control, and "upgrade" any form of technology.
