DIAMOND HEAD
Species: Petrosapien

Diamondhead is a Petrosapien from the crystal planet Petropia. His body is made of extremely durable crystal that can refract energy attacks, form weapons, and regenerate when damaged. He is one of Ben’s most reliable combat aliens.
